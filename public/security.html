Security & data handling

Read-only by default. Nothing touches your controls.

LeanFM analyzes a copy of trend data your building automation system already produces. We don't connect to your control network, we don't write setpoints, and your data is used only to run your analysis. Here's exactly how that works.

ACCESS

Read-only, no control writes

We work from a trend-data export or a read-only connection. OnPoint never sends commands to your BAS and never changes a setpoint, schedule, or sequence. Your team makes every change.

USE

Your data, for your analysis

Trend data you share is used to perform your analysis and produce your findings. It is not sold, and it is not used to train models shared with other customers without your written agreement.

DATA

What we collect

Equipment trend logs — temperatures, setpoints, valve and damper positions, runtimes, meter data. Operational telemetry about equipment, not personal data about people.

HOSTING

Hosting & access control

Data is stored with a reputable cloud provider, encrypted in transit and at rest, with access limited to the LeanFM team working on your engagement. Specifics are in our security documentation.

RETENTION

Retention & deletion

We retain your data for the life of the engagement so AIR can track change over time. On request, or at the end of the engagement, we delete it. Your data handling is governed by your agreement.

PEOPLE

Human review

Findings are reviewed by LeanFM engineers before they reach you. The same access controls and confidentiality apply to everyone who touches your data.

For IT & procurement: we're glad to complete your security questionnaire and share our current data-handling documentation, hosting details, and connection options. The fastest path is a short call with your facilities and IT leads together.
