The OnPoint platform

One platform, from data to decision.

OnPoint connects to the building automation system you already run, reads its trend data, and turns it into a score, an advisor, ranked findings, and reports your team can act on. No new sensors, no new hardware, nothing touching your control sequences.

01 — What's inside

Four parts, one job: turn data you have into money you keep.

THE SOFTWARE

OnPoint

The score, the findings, the advisor, and the reports — together in one place, connected read-only to your BAS.

 THE ENGINE

Prescriptiv

The fault-detection AI, developed from Carnegie Mellon research, that screens your trend data for the faults alarms can't see.

 THE SCORE →

AIR

One 0–100 rating across energy, comfort, maintenance risk, and controls health — every rating opens to its evidence.

 THE ADVISOR →

Maple

Ask your building anything and get an engineer's answer — diagnosis, evidence, recommended action, estimated impact.
